Below is information on the two places I love to visit.

Museum of Contemporary Art

The MCA has collected and exhibited work by living artists since its founding in 1967, the musuem houses more than 12,000 works of art across a broad range of media from 1900 to the present.MCA is located in downtown Chicago, an is one of the largest contemporary art venues.

Corbett vs. Dempsey

Opening in 2004 Corbett vs. Dempsey has been committed to the historical and contemporary legacy of Chicago art, and to the task of presenting major international artists in the Windy City.
