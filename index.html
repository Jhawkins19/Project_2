Art Culture

The ever-growing Chicago art scence is fun, inviting and diverse and often times free to residents. There's an array of galleries, exhibits, standing sculputres, performance art shows,local venues, and even community art-walks.

I've had the opputunity to visit many different art events of all kinds, contemporary to graffiti events and there's so much artist diversity in Chicago I truly believe that there is something here for everyone to love and be inspired by when checking out Chicago's art culture.

The Musuem of Contemporary Art Chicago

-Also known as MCA Chicago

MCA Chicago is a contemporary art musuem located in 220 E Chicago Ave, in the downtown Chicago area. This musuem is gallery-style, Which I really enjoy it makes your experience more personal and you forget that your in a local musuem. This is a beautiful facility, with great architure and design. Itshard not to be creativily inspired when visting MCA Chicago.
